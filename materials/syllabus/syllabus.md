# Computer Information — Official Syllabus (extracted)

## Course metadata

Course:

Prerequisite: Knowledge of basic computing.

Semester:

Rationale: This course is designed to develop essential computer and Artificial Intelligence skills among law students for effective legal documentation, research, data management, and professional presentations. It also promotes ethical and responsible use of AI technologies in modern legal practice.

SEE - Semester End Examination, CIA - Continuous Internal Assessment (It consists of Assignments/Seminars/Presentations/MCQ Tests, etc.)
